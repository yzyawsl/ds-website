Welcome to My Website

Hi! I'm a data enthusiast exploring visualization, analysis, and human–data interaction. This personal site is a living notebook for class projects, experiments with D3, and reflections on the craft of transforming raw data into clear, engaging stories. In DS4200, I focus on building explainable, reproducible visuals—whether it’s a tidy line chart or an interactive dashboard. My interests include visual encodings, perceptual design, and telling honest stories with data (no chartjunk!).

Beyond classwork, I love prototyping with web technologies and performing end‑to‑end workflows: wrangling CSV/JSON, designing effective scales and legends, and polishing annotation layers that guide the reader’s attention. This site highlights several exercises, such as box plots, side‑by‑side bars, and time‑series lines. You’ll also find notes about deployment and accessibility, including semantic HTML, keyboard‑friendly interactions, and color‑contrast checks.
